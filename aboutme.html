About Me
ANNA-KAISA JUHOLA

Eurajoki, Finland

annakaisa.juhola (at) gmail.com

NICE TO MEET YOU!

I am a student of Information and Communication Technology, with a strong passion for software engineering and web design. My focus lies in creating both websites and software, encompassing both front-end and back-end development.

Before transitioning to the tech field, I had a successful career as an jeweler entrepreneur. As a goldsmith and hand engraver, I specialized in designing unique jewelry pieces, particularly engagement and wedding rings. This background honed my creative skills and attention to detail, which I now bring into my work in technology.

I am a blend of creativity and logic, constantly seeking ways to merge artistic expression with technical precision. In my free time, I enjoy various crafts and art forms, including painting and ceramics, as well as spending time in nature. I am an avid mushroom forager and deeply connected to the natural world, with a special love for animals—cats, in particular.

I also have a passion for reading and listening to audiobooks, which fuels my curiosity and love for learning, but is also a great way to relax.

I am enthusiastic about continuous learning and applying knowledge in practical and innovative ways. My diverse skill set and enthusiasm for acquiring new knowledge make me a versatile and committed individual in everything I pursue. My motto is: Anything I don't know (yet), I can learn.

With love,

Kaisa
